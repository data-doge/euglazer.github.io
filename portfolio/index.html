Expanding + Testing a Computational Method for Predicting the E°red of Organic Molecules on the Basis of Correlation to Experiment [2011]

Date/Organization

Used molecular modeling software to (1) test + expand a correlation between the E°red and computed D0 - S0 energy difference of a set of organic compounds, (2) further develope a procedure for predicting the E°red of our lab's synthesized photochromes using this correlation, and (3) determine future synthetic targets based on these predictions.
