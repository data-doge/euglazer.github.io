About

Occasionally, I'll stop working and take a look at other people's work. I'll go to a hackerspace, an art exhibit, a show, or watch shit on the internet. Sometimes, I'll come across something that is just so fucking perfect and there's this moment where I fall in love and my skin becomes pinpricks and chemicals flood my brain and ideas start flowing and flowing and flowing. I love these moments, and so I try my hardest to create them for other people. I've a background in computational chemistry, microfluidics, embedded programming, circuit design, mechanical part design, and recently haptics + augmented reality. In my free time, I build electronic musical instruments and interactive, multimedia art installations. Now, I am studying web development.
